Keyphrases are the essential topical phrases that summarize a document. In this paper, we investigate the zero-shot capabilities of open-source instruction-tuned LLMs (Phi-3, Llama-3) and GPT-4o for keyphrase generation. We systematically study the effect of specialized instructions and design task-specific multi-sample aggregation strategies, showing significant improvements over baselines.
